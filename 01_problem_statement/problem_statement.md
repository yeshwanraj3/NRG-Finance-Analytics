# Business Problem

Nordic Retail Group (NRG) operates in the home and lifestyle retail segment.
The company faces challenges in profitability analysis, cost visibility, 
and understanding monthly financial performance.

Current reporting is limited:
- Profit trends are unclear
- Costs are not fully attributed
- Scenario analysis is not available
- Account mapping are not properly alligned

Objective: Build an analytics solution to improve profitability, reduce costs, 
and provide clear, actionable insights.
